Tribute page to Dr.Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian who led initiatives that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution.

Honors and Awards

Selection of Honors and Awards

  • 1968: A street in Ciudad Obregón was named after Borlaug.
  • 1970: Honorary doctorate given by the Agricultural University of Norway.
  • 1975: Borlaug was named a Distinguished Fellow of the Iowa Academy of Science.
  • 1977: Received the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 1977: Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 1980: Elected honorary member of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences.
  • 1986: Borlaug was inducted into the Scandinavian-American Hall of Fame during Norsk Høstfest.
  • 2002: Received the Public Welfare Medal from the National Academy of Sciences.
